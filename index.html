Most organisations complete their initial self-audit and have a working legal register within a single working day. Our onboarding team supports you through setup and you're not left to figure it out alone.

iCOR is built specifically for ISO 14001 (Environmental Management) and ISO 45001 (Occupational Health & Safety). We cover the specific clauses organisations struggle with most — including 6.1.3, 6.1.4, 9.1.2, and 10.2.

iCOR works for teams of any size. One EHS manager can run it solo, or you can invite your full team and assign actions to relevant colleagues. Access levels mean people only see what's relevant to them.

Our EHS legal specialists continuously monitor and update the regulation library. When legislation changes, you're notified and can update your compliance status — you no longer need to track regulatory changes yourself.
